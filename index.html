About Sneh

I moved to Cedar Falls when I was 11 years old and have lived there since. I am currently a junior at the University of Iowa studying both Finance and Business Analytics with an expected gradutation of May 2025.

Throughout my college life, I have been a part of various organizations with leaderships roles in many of the clubs. Currently, I am a member of the Tippie Real Estate Club, South Asian Student Alliance, and Tippie Tehcnology and Innovation Association. I serve as the president of the Tippie Tehcnology and Innovation Association for this school year and it has been a great learning experience for me.

My professional experience includes working in the hospitatity industry throughout my life. Last summer, I interned under Kristy Walker on a business analytics team and worked with a local Iowa City business district to help them understand ways in which they can drive more traffic in the area. This upcoming summer, I will be interning in Minneapolis at RSM as a Risk Technology Consultant.
